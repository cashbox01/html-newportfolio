The Best Movies according to Fidel

My Top Three Movies of all-time

2001 Space Odysey

Based on the novel of the same name written by Arthur C. Clark, Stanley Kubrick directed this astonishing film about human origins, technology and final encounter with creators.

Fellini 8 1/2

In this film Italian director Federico Fellini tells us about the creative process, how it is influenced by our memories and current events. To Fellini we come to earth to take a rol in the life scenery until life ends.

Blade Runner

Ridley Scott based on Philip K. Dick novel Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?give us the story of four replicants in search of their creator in a post-apocaliptic LA.
